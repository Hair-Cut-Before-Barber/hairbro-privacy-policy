Privacy Policy for HairBro

Effective Date: 3 July 2025

HairBro (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the HairBro app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Face Data: We use your device’s camera to detect your face shape and features. This information is used only to recommend haircuts and help you align your face in the camera view. Your face data for these features never leaves your device.
  • Photos for Hairstyle Swap: If you use the hairstyle swap feature, your selfie is securely uploaded to our server for processing. Your image is used only to create your hairstyle result and is deleted immediately after processing. We never keep your photos or face data after the process is finished.
  • Reference Photos: If you upload your own photo to try on a hairstyle, this photo is stored only while you are using the app. It is automatically deleted when you close the app, and is never shared or stored beyond your session.
  • Local Photo Storage: You can save your captured or uploaded photos on your device to easily view your previous results. These photos are stored only on your device, never shared or uploaded, and can be deleted at any time by clearing the app’s cache or uninstalling the app.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, non-personal usage data to help us improve the app. No face or photo data is included in these statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To suggest haircuts and guide you to align your face in the camera (processed only on your device)
  • To generate hairstyle swaps (your photo is processed securely and deleted right after use)
  • To let you try on hairstyles using your own reference images (photos are deleted when you close the app)
  • To save your photos locally for your convenience (never leaves your device unless you save to your gallery)
  • To improve your experience and app functionality through anonymous usage statistics

3. Face Data Handling & Retention

  • No face data or photos are ever stored permanently on our servers.
  • Analysis for recommendations and camera alignment happens only on your device and is never sent anywhere else.
  • When you use the hairstyle swap, your image is sent securely, processed, and deleted immediately after your result is created (usually within seconds). We never keep your photos longer than necessary to create your hairstyle result.
  • Reference photos for try-on are stored only while you use the app and deleted as soon as you close it.
  • Photos saved for your convenience remain only on your device and can be deleted at any time.
  • We never use your face data or photos for identification, tracking, or marketing purposes.
  • If you choose to save a hairstyle result or try-on photo, it will be added to your device’s photo gallery. This happens only if you request it, and you can delete these photos any time in your gallery app.

4. Data Security

  • All photo transfers are protected with secure encryption.
  • Photos sent for processing are deleted immediately after use and never linked to your identity.
  • Photos stored on your device are never sent or shared by the app.
  • We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • You can use all local features without uploading any photos.
  • You can delete saved photos and reference images at any time by clearing the app’s cache or uninstalling the app.
  • You choose if and when to save photos to your device’s gallery. You can delete these photos anytime in your gallery.
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all data stored on your device.
  • Contact us at filzaramadhan2003@gmail.com for any privacy questions or concerns.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Please check this page regularly. The latest update date is shown at the top.

For questions or support, contact us at filzaramadhan2003@gmail.com.

Kebijakan Privasi HairBro (Bahasa Indonesia)

Tanggal Berlaku: 3 Juli 2025

HairBro (“kami”) menghargai privasi Anda. Kebijakan Privasi ini menjelaskan bagaimana kami mengumpulkan, menggunakan, dan melindungi data Anda saat menggunakan aplikasi HairBro.

1. Data yang Kami Kumpulkan

  • Data Wajah: Kami menggunakan kamera perangkat Anda untuk mendeteksi bentuk dan fitur wajah. Data ini hanya digunakan untuk merekomendasikan gaya rambut dan membantu Anda membingkai wajah di kamera. Data wajah untuk fitur ini tidak pernah keluar dari perangkat Anda.
  • Foto untuk Swap Gaya Rambut: Jika Anda menggunakan fitur swap rambut, foto Anda dikirim dengan aman ke server kami hanya untuk proses hasil rambut. Foto Anda hanya digunakan untuk membuat hasil gaya rambut dan langsung dihapus setelah diproses. Kami tidak pernah menyimpan foto atau data wajah Anda setelah proses selesai.
  • Foto Referensi: Jika Anda mengunggah foto sendiri untuk mencoba gaya rambut, foto ini hanya disimpan sementara selama aplikasi dibuka. Foto otomatis dihapus saat aplikasi ditutup dan tidak pernah dibagikan atau disimpan setelah itu.
  • Penyimpanan Foto Lokal: Anda dapat menyimpan foto hasil atau foto yang diunggah di perangkat agar mudah dilihat kembali. Foto hanya tersimpan di perangkat Anda, tidak pernah diunggah, dan dapat dihapus kapan saja dengan menghapus cache aplikasi atau uninstall aplikasi.
  • Data Penggunaan Aplikasi: Kami dapat mengumpulkan data penggunaan anonim (bukan data pribadi) untuk meningkatkan aplikasi. Tidak ada data wajah atau foto yang termasuk dalam statistik ini.

  • Untuk menyimpan foto di perangkat Anda untuk kenyamanan (tidak pernah keluar dari perangkat kecuali Anda simpan ke galeri)
