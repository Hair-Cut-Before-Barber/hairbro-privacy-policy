Privacy Policy for HairBro

Effective Date: 3 July 2025

HairBro (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the HairBro app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Face Data: We use your device camera and Apple’s Vision framework to detect your face shape and facial features directly on your device. This information is used only to recommend haircuts and help you align your face in the camera view. No face data leaves your device for these features.
  • Photos for Hairstyle Swap: When you use the hairstyle swap feature, your selfie is securely uploaded to our server for processing. The image is used only to create your result and is deleted immediately after processing. No face data or photos are kept on our server after your hairstyle has been generated.
  • Reference Photos: If you upload your own reference image to try on a hairstyle, this image is stored only temporarily while you use the app. It is automatically deleted when you close the app, and is never shared or stored beyond your session.
  • Local Photo Storage: Your captured or uploaded photos may be saved on your device, so you can access your previous results more easily. These photos are kept only on your device, never shared or uploaded, and can be deleted at any time by clearing the app cache or uninstalling the app.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, non-personal usage data to improve the app. No face or photo data is ever included in these statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze your face shape and recommend hairstyles (processed only on your device)
  • To provide AI-powered hairstyle swaps (image processing is temporary and deleted immediately after use)
  • To let you try on styles from your own reference images (deleted automatically when the app is closed)
  • To save your photos locally for your convenience (never leaves your device)
  • To improve your experience and app functionality through anonymous usage statistics

3. Face Data Handling & Retention

  • No face data or images are stored permanently on our servers.
  • Face analysis for recommendations and camera alignment happens directly on your device and is never sent or stored anywhere else.
  • When you use the hairstyle swap feature, your image is securely sent to our server, processed, and deleted immediately after your hairstyle result is created (usually within seconds). We never retain your images longer than necessary to deliver your result.
  • Reference photos you upload for try-on are held only temporarily during your session and are automatically deleted when you close the app.
  • Photos saved for your convenience remain only on your device and can be deleted at any time by clearing the app’s cache or uninstalling the app.
  • We never use face data or photos for identification, tracking, or marketing purposes.
  • If you choose to save a hairstyle result or try-on photo, it will be added to your device’s photo gallery. This happens only if you request it, and you remain in control of deleting these photos at any time through your gallery app.

4. Data Security

  • All photo transfers to our server are protected with secure encryption (HTTPS).
  • Images sent to our server for processing are deleted automatically after use and never linked to your identity.
  • Photos saved on your device are never sent or shared by the app.
  • We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • You can use all local features (face detection, recommendations) without uploading any photos.
  • You can delete saved photos and reference images at any time by clearing the app’s cache or uninstalling the app.
  • You choose if and when to save try-on photos to your device’s gallery. You can delete these photos anytime from your photo library.
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all data stored on your device.
  • Contact us at filzaramadhan2003@gmail.com for any privacy questions or concerns.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Please review this page regularly. The latest update date is shown at the top of this policy.

For questions or support, contact us at filzaramadhan2003@gmail.com.
